Biryani

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cubed)
  • Basmati Rice
  • Onion and Tomato
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani Masala (or garam masala)
  • Whole Spices (cloves, cardamoms, bay leaf, cinnamon)
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander

Steps

  1. Marinate Paneer: In a bowl, mix paneer cubes with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and salt. Let it sit for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices (cloves, cardamom, bay leaf, cinnamon) until 70-80% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. Sauté Paneer: In a pan, heat oil or ghee and lightly sauté the marinated paneer, then remove and set aside.
  4. Prepare Biryani Base: In the same pan, sauté onions until golden, add tomatoes, and cook with spices (like cumin, coriander powder, and biryani masala) until fragrant. Add veggies if desired.
  5. Layer: In a large pot, layer the paneer mixture, then rice, and sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron-soaked milk.
  6. Dum Cooking: Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  7. Serve: Fluff gently, garnish, and enjoy your flavorful paneer biryani!
