GulabJamun

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2–3 tbsp milk (add as needed to make dough)
  • 1 ½ cups sugar 1 ½ cups water
  • 3–4 cardamom pods (slightly crushed)

Steps

  1. Syrup: Boil 1.5 cups sugar with 1.5 cups water, add 3-4 crushed cardamom pods, and simmer for 8–10 minutes. Add rose water if desired.
  2. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup flour, 1/4 tsp baking soda, and 2 tbsp ghee. Add milk gradually to form a soft, smooth dough.
  3. and Fry: Make small, smooth balls from the dough. Heat oil on low-medium and fry the balls until golden brown.
  4. Transfer fried balls to warm syrup and let them soak for 2–3 hours. Enjoy!
